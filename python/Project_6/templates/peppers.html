The 5 Genius of peppers (Capsicum)

  1. 1. Capsicum Annuum
  2. 2. Capsicum Chinense
  3. 3. Capsicum Frutescens
  4. 4. Capsicum Pubescens
  5. 5. Capsicum Baccatum
  • some types of Annuum peppers are: Bell peppers, Jalapeno and Cayenne
  • some types of Chinense peppers are: Habanero and Scoth bonnet
  • some types of Frutescens peppers are: tabasco and malagueta
  • A type of Pubescens pepper is: Manzano (they are rare)
  • some types of Baccatum peppers are: Aji amarillo and Aji pineapple
